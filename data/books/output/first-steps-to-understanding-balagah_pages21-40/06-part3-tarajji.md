# Part 3 (cont.) -- Type 5: تَرَجٍّ (Tarajji - Hoping)

**Unit 1: علم المعاني (Ilm al-Ma'ani)**
**Page: 29**

---

## Primary Usage of تَرَجٍّ

تَرَجٍّ is to express desire for something possible.

The following two words are used for this:

### 1) عَسٰى

<div dir="rtl">

> ﴿عَسَى اللّٰهُ أَنْ يَّأْتِيَنِيْ بِهِمْ جَمِيْعًا﴾

</div>

*Hopefully, Allah may bring them all together.*

### 2) لَعَلَّ

<div dir="rtl">

> ﴿لَا تَدْرِيْ لَعَلَّ اللّٰهَ يُحْدِثُ بَعْدَ ذٰلِكَ أَمْرًا﴾

</div>

*It may be that Allah brings about a new situation thereafter.*

---

## Secondary Uses of تَرَجٍّ and تَمَنٍّ

The particles of تَرَجٍّ and تَمَنٍّ can be interchanged.

<div dir="rtl">

> ﴿وَقَالَ فِرْعَوْنُ يَٓاَيُّهَا الْمَلَأُ مَا عَلِمْتُ لَكُمْ مِنْ اِلٰهٍ غَيْرِيْ فَأَوْقِدْ لِيْ يٰهَامٰنُ عَلَى الطِّيْنِ فَاجْعَلْ لِّيْ صَرْحًا لَعَلِّيْٓ
> أَطَّلِعُ إِلٰٓى اِلٰهِ مُوسٰى وَإِنِّيْ لَأَظُنُّهُ مِنَ الْكٰذِبِيْنَ﴾

</div>

*And Fir'aun (Pharaoh) said, "O Haman, make a tower for me, perhaps I could reach the ways - The ways to the heavens, and peek towards the God of Musa.*

Even though this is something impossible, Fir'aun expressed this idea giving the impression to those around him that it was possible. This was then expressed in the Quran using the word لَعَلَّ.

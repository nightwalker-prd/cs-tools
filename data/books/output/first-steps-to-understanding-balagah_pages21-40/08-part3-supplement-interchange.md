# Part 3 (cont.) -- Supplement: Interchanging جُمْلَةٌ إِنْشَائِيَّةٌ and جُمْلَةٌ خَبَرِيَّةٌ

**Unit 1: علم المعاني (Ilm al-Ma'ani)**
**Pages: 33-34**

---

A جُمْلَةٌ إِنْشَائِيَّةٌ and a جُمْلَةٌ خَبَرِيَّةٌ can be interchanged.

---

## Using a خَبَرِيَّةٌ in Place of an أَمْرٌ and نَهْيٌ

In an أَمْرٌ or نَهْيٌ, the addressee is issued an instruction or prohibition. He then has the choice to carry it out or not.

In a خَبَرِيَّةٌ, a statement is issued regarding something that is going to be done or not going to be done as a matter of fact.

Therefore, by expressing a command as a خَبَرِيَّةٌ more emphasis is created.

This feature can occur with a اِسْمِيَّةٌ or فِعْلِيَّةٌ.

### Example 1 (فِعْلِيَّةٌ)

<div dir="rtl">

> ﴿وَاِذْ اَخَذْنَا مِيْثَاقَكُمْ لَا تَسْفِكُوْنَ دِمَآءَكُمْ وَلَا تُخْرِجُوْنَ اَنْفُسَكُمْ مِنْ دِيَارِكُمْ ثُمَّ اَقْرَرْتُمْ
> وَاَنْتُمْ تَشْهَدُوْنَ﴾

</div>

*When We took a pledge from you: "You shall not shed the blood of one another, and you shall not drive one another out of your homes."*

### Example 2 (اِسْمِيَّةٌ)

<div dir="rtl">

> ﴿فَمَنْ فَرَضَ فِيْهِنَّ الْحَجَّ فَلَا رَفَثَ وَلَا فُسُوْقَ وَلَا جِدَالَ فِي الْحَجِّ﴾

</div>

*There should be no obscenity, no sin, and no quarrel in Hajj.*

---

## Using an إِنْشَائِيَّةٌ in Place of a خَبَرِيَّةٌ

An إِنْشَائِيَّةٌ may also be used in place of a خَبَرِيَّةٌ.

<div dir="rtl">

> ﴿قُلْ اَمَرَ رَبِّيْ بِالْقِسْطِ وَاَقِيْمُوْا وُجُوْهَكُمْ عِنْدَ كُلِّ مَسْجِدٍ وَادْعُوْهُ مُخْلِصِيْنَ لَهُ الدِّيْنَ﴾

</div>

*My Lord has commanded justice and that you should face Him only [during prayers] in every place of worship.*

In this verse two commands are issued: to be just and to turn towards Allah at the time of prayer. However, the second command is expressed via an إِنْشَائِيَّةٌ, in a style different to the first. This creates an emphasis on the second command.

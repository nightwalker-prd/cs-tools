# Part 5 -- Details About نَكِرَةٌ and مَعْرِفَةٌ

**Unit 1: علم المعاني (Ilm al-Ma'ani)**
**Pages: 39-40**

---

A noun can be either نَكِرَةٌ or مَعْرِفَةٌ.

In this discussion we will touch on a few rules regarding the six types of مَعْرِفَةٌ and some rules about نَكِرَةٌ.

---

## ضَمِيرٌ (Damir - Pronouns)

Below are some rules regarding the use of ضَمِيرٌ, pronouns.

### الإِضْمَارُ مَقَامَ الإِظْهَارِ (Using a Pronoun in Place of a Noun)

A 3rd person pronoun, ضَمِيرُ الْغَائِبِ, is only used after an antecedent. However, a ضَمِيرُ الْغَائِبِ is sometimes used without an antecedent. One reason for this is to highlight that the noun is well known and understood by all, and therefore does not need to be mentioned to be understood.

<div dir="rtl">

> ﴿الٓمٓ ۞ تَنْزِيلُ الْكِتٰبِ لَا رَيْبَ فِيْهِ مِنْ رَبِّ الْعٰلَمِيْنَ ۞ أَمْ يَقُولُونَ افْتَرٰىهُ ۚ بَلْ هُوَ الْحَقُّ مِنْ
> رَبِّكَ لِتُنْذِرَ قَوْمًا مَّآ أَتٰهُمْ مِنْ نَذِيرٍ مِنْ قَبْلِكَ لَعَلَّهُمْ يَهْتَدُوْنَ﴾

</div>

*(This is) revelation of the Book - in which there is no doubt - is from the Lord of the worlds. Is it that they (the disbelievers) say, "He (Muhammad, peace and blessings be upon him) has fabricated it."*

### الإِظْهَارُ مَقَامَ الإِضْمَارِ (Repeating a Noun Instead of Using a Pronoun)

After a noun has been mentioned, it is consequently referred to as a pronoun. However, sometimes the noun can be repeated. One reason for this is to highlight the grandeur of that thing.

<div dir="rtl">

> ﴿اَلْقَارِعَةُ ۞ مَا الْقَارِعَةُ ۞ وَمَآ اَدْرٰىكَ مَا الْقَارِعَةُ﴾

</div>

*The Striking Event! What is the Striking Event? And what may let you know what the Striking Event is?*

Even though the word الْقَارِعَةُ has been mentioned previously, it is repeated as a noun and not replaced with its pronoun هِيَ. This highlights the grandeur of الْقَارِعَةُ.

## الْتِفَاتٌ (Iltifat - Shifting Pronouns)

الْتِفَاتٌ is to change the person in repeated pronouns. For example, to change from 1st person to 2nd person.

### Example: 1st to 2nd person

<div dir="rtl">

> ﴿وَمَا لِيَ لَآ اَعْبُدُ الَّذِيْ فَطَرَنِيْ وَاِلَيْهِ تُرْجَعُوْنَ﴾

</div>

*What excuse do I have if I do not worship the One who has created me and to whom you will be returned?*

In the above verse, the pronouns change from 1st person to 2nd person.

### Example: 3rd to 2nd person

<div dir="rtl">

> عَبَسَ وَتَوَلّٰى ۞ أَنْ جَآءَهُ الْأَعْمٰى ۞ وَمَا يُدْرِيكَ لَعَلَّهُ يَزَّكّٰى ۞ أَوْ يَذَّكَّرُ
> فَتَنْفَعَهُ الذِّكْرٰى ۞ أَمَّا مَنِ اسْتَغْنٰى ۞ فَأَنْتَ لَهُ تَصَدّٰى ۞

</div>

In the above verse, the pronouns change from 3rd person to 2nd person.

## Note

The مُخَاطَبٌ pronoun can be used to make a general reference to any person, not just the person being spoken to.

<div dir="rtl">

> «ذِكْرُكَ أَخَاكَ بِمَا يَكْرَهُ»

</div>

*[Backbiting] is your talking about your brother in a manner which he does not like.*

In this hadith, the pronoun "you" does not refer to a single addressee, rather it applies to any person who hears or reads it.

## Introduction (Page 90)

The sub-science of عِلْمُ الْبَدِيْعِ deals with the aspects of Arabic which are related to embellishments; features that beautify the words or meaning of the sentence. These are called مُحَسَّنَاتٌ.

There are two types of مُحَسَّنَاتٌ:

1. مُحَسَّنَاتٌ مَعْنَوِيَّةٌ: embellishments related to meaning
2. مُحَسَّنَاتٌ لَفْظِيَّةٌ: embellishments related to words

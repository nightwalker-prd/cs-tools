# Unit 2: عِلْمُ الْبَيَان (Ilm al-Bayan) — الْمَجَازُ الْعَقْلِيُّ (Al-Majaz al-'Aqli) & الْمَجَازُ الاِشْتِقَاقِيُّ (Al-Majaz al-Ishtiqaqi)

*Page 87*

---

## الْمَجَازُ الْعَقْلِيُّ (Al-Majaz al-'Aqli — Intellectual/Rational Metaphor)

<div dir="rtl">

الْمَجَازُ الْعَقْلِيُّ

</div>

الْمَجَازُ الْعَقْلِيُّ is an اِسْتِعَارَةٌ in the link between the مُسْنَدٌ and مُسْنَدٌ اِلَيْهِ. i.e., to attribute the فِعْلٌ to someone or something that is not its actual فَاعِلٌ.

**Quranic Example:**

<div dir="rtl">

﴿اِنَّ فِرْعَوْنَ عَلَا فِي الْأَرْضِ ... يُذَبِّحُ اَبْنَاءَهُمْ﴾

</div>

> *Indeed, Fir'awn had become high-handed in the land ... **slaughtering** their sons*

Fir'awn is described as slaughtering the sons of the Banu Israel. Although he may not have physically carried out the act himself, it is ascribed to him due to his command to his army to do so.

## Supplement: الْمَجَازُ الاِشْتِقَاقِيُّ (Al-Majaz al-Ishtiqaqi)

<div dir="rtl">

الْمَجَازُ الاِشْتِقَاقِيُّ

</div>

الْمَجَازُ الْعَقْلِيُّ can also occur in مُشْتَقَّاتٌ i.e. one مُشْتَقٌّ can be used in the meaning of another.

---

### اِسْمُ فَاعِلٍ in the Meaning of اِسْمُ مَفْعُوْلٍ

An اِسْمُ فَاعِلٍ can carry the meaning of an اِسْمُ مَفْعُوْلٍ.

**Quranic Example:**

<div dir="rtl">

﴿فَهُوَ فِيْ عِيْشَةٍ رَّاضِيَةٍ ۞﴾

</div>

> *He will be in an **enjoyable** life.*

In this verse, the رَاضِيَةٌ of اِسْمُ الْفَاعِلِ (happy) is used in the meaning of the اِسْمُ الْمَفْعُوْلِ i.e., مَرْضِيَّةٌ (enjoyable).

---

### اِسْمُ مَفْعُوْلٍ in the Meaning of اِسْمُ فَاعِلٍ

An اِسْمُ مَفْعُوْلٍ can carry the meaning of an اِسْمُ فَاعِلٍ.

**Quranic Example:**

<div dir="rtl">

﴿حِجَابًا مَّسْتُوْرًا ۞﴾

</div>

> *A covering veil*

In this verse, the مَسْتُوْرٌ of اِسْمُ الْمَفْعُوْلِ (covered) is used in the meaning of the اِسْمُ الْفَاعِلِ i.e., سَاتِرٌ (covering).

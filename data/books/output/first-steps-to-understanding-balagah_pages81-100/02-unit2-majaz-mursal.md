# Unit 2: عِلْمُ الْبَيَان (Ilm al-Bayan) — الْمَجَازُ الْمُرْسَلُ (Al-Majaz al-Mursal)

*Pages 84-86*

---

## Definition

<div dir="rtl">

الْمَجَازُ الْمُرْسَلُ

</div>

A الْمَجَازُ الْمُرْسَلُ is a type of figurative language where the relationship between the literal meaning of the word and the intended meaning is not based on تَشْبِيْهٌ, rather on something closely related to the literal meaning.

Below are some of the عَلَاقَة of مَجَازٌ مُرْسَلٌ.

---

## 1. سَبَبِيَّةٌ (Sababiyyah — Causality) (Page 84)

سَبَبِيَّةٌ is an عَلَاقَةٌ in which the literal meaning is the **cause** of the metaphorical meaning.

**Hadith Example:**

<div dir="rtl">

«اَلْمُسْلِمُ مَنْ سَلِمَ الْمُسْلِمُوْنَ مِنْ لِسَانِهِ وَيَدِهِ»

</div>

> *A Muslim is the one from whose **tongue** and **hands** Muslims are safe.*

### Diagram: لِسَانٌ

| Literal Meaning | عَلَاقَة | Metaphorical Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| tongue | سَبَبِيَّةٌ (cause) | words |

### Diagram: يَدٌ (hands)

| Literal Meaning | عَلَاقَة | Metaphorical Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| hands | سَبَبِيَّةٌ (cause) | actions |

---

## 2. مُسَبَّبِيَّةٌ (Musabbabiyyah — Result) (Page 84)

مُسَبَّبِيَّةٌ is an عَلَاقَةٌ in which the literal meaning is the **result** of the metaphorical meaning.

**Quranic Example:**

<div dir="rtl">

﴿وَيُنَزِّلُ لَكُمْ مِّنَ السَّمَآءِ رِزْقًا﴾

</div>

> *And He sends down **provision** for you from the sky.*

### Diagram: رِزْقٌ

| Literal Meaning | عَلَاقَة | Metaphorical Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| sustenance | مُسَبَّبِيَّةٌ (result) | rain |

---

## 3. كُلِّيَّةٌ (Kulliyyah — Whole) (Page 85)

كُلِّيَّةٌ is an عَلَاقَةٌ in which the literal meaning is the **whole** of the metaphorical meaning.

**Quranic Example:**

<div dir="rtl">

﴿يَجْعَلُوْنَ اَصَابِعَهُمْ فِيْٓ اٰذَانِهِمْ﴾

</div>

> *They thrust their **fingers** in their ears.*

### Diagram: أَصَابِعُ

| Literal Meaning | عَلَاقَة | Metaphorical Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| fingers | كُلِّيَّةٌ (whole) | fingertips |

---

## 4. جُزْئِيَّةٌ (Juz'iyyah — Part) (Page 85)

جُزْئِيَّةٌ is an عَلَاقَةٌ in which the literal meaning is a **portion** of the metaphorical meaning.

**Quranic Example:**

<div dir="rtl">

﴿اَوْ تَحْرِيْرُ رَقَبَةٍ﴾

</div>

> *Freeing of a **neck**.*

### Diagram: رَقَبَةٌ

| Literal Meaning | عَلَاقَة | Metaphorical Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| neck | جُزْئِيَّةٌ (part) | slave |

---

## 5. اِعْتِبَارُ مَا كَانَ (I'tibar Ma Kan — Past State) (Page 85)

اِعْتِبَارُ مَا كَانَ is an عَلَاقَةٌ in which the literal meaning is the **past state** of the metaphorical meaning.

**Quranic Example:**

<div dir="rtl">

﴿وَاٰتُوا الْيَتٰمٰى اَمْوَالَهُمْ وَلَا تَتَبَدَّلُوا الْخَبِيْثَ بِالطَّيِّبِ﴾

</div>

> *Give the **orphans** their property.*

### Diagram: يَتَامٰى

| Literal Meaning | عَلَاقَة | Metaphorical Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| orphan | اِعْتِبَارُ مَا كَانَ (past state) | adult who was an orphan |

---

## 6. اِعْتِبَارُ مَا يَكُوْنُ (I'tibar Ma Yakun — Future State) (Page 86)

اِعْتِبَارُ مَا يَكُوْنُ is an عَلَاقَةٌ in which the literal meaning is the **future state** of the metaphorical meaning.

**Quranic Example:**

<div dir="rtl">

﴿قَالَ اَحَدُهُمَآ اِنِّيْٓ اَرٰنِيْٓ اَعْصِرُ خَمْرًا﴾

</div>

> *I have seen myself [in dream] pressing **wine**.*

### Diagram: خَمْرٌ

| Literal Meaning | عَلَاقَة | Metaphorical Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| wine | اِعْتِبَارُ مَا يَكُوْنُ (future state) | grapes |

---

## 7. مُقَارَبَةٌ (Muqarabah — Proximity) (Page 86)

مُقَارَبَةٌ is an عَلَاقَةٌ in which the literal meaning is something **near in time or space** to the metaphorical meaning.

**Hadith Example:**

<div dir="rtl">

مَنْ تَرَكَهَا (الصَّلَاةَ) فَقَدْ كَفَرَ

</div>

> *Whoever leaves salah intentionally has (come close to) **disbelieving**.*

### Diagram: كَفَرَ

| Literal Meaning | عَلَاقَة | Metaphorical Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| becomes a disbeliever | قُرْبٌ (proximity) | came close to becoming a disbeliever |

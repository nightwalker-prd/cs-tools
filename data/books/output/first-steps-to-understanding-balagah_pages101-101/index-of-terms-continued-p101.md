## Closing Supplication — دعاء كفارة المجلس

The book concludes with the well-known *Kaffārat al-Majlis* (Expiation of the Gathering) supplication:

<div dir="rtl">

> سُبْحَانَكَ اللَّهُمَّ وَبِحَمْدِكَ
>
> أَشْهَدُ أَنْ لَا إِلٰهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ
>
> أَسْتَغْفِرُكَ وَأَتُوبُ إِلَيْكَ

</div>

**Translation:**

> Glory be to You, O Allah, and praise be to You.
>
> I bear witness that there is no deity worthy of worship except You.
>
> I seek Your forgiveness and I repent to You.

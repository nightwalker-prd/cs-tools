# Unit 1: عِلْمُ الْمَعَانِي — 'Ilm al-Ma'ani

*(Pages 7–8)*

## Introduction

The sub-science of عِلْمُ الْمَعَانِي deals with the aspects of Arabic language that are related to the meaning of words, sentences, and their structures. This unit will cover eight topics related to عِلْمُ الْمَعَانِي:

1. Parts of a Sentence
2. Types of Sentences
3. Word Order
4. Details of نَكِرَةٌ and مَعْرِفَةٌ
5. Restrictions
6. Joining Sentences
7. Length of Speech
8. Miscellaneous Rules

### Who is this book for?

The purpose of this book is to act as an introductory guide to the study of Balagah, the science of eloquence in Arabic.

Its target audience is students who have already acquired a foundation in the Arabic language, specifically in areas such as Nahw (grammar), Sarf (morphology), and Literature.

# Part 6 -- القَصْرُ (Qasr - Restriction)

**Unit 1: علم المعاني (Ilm al-Ma'ani)**
**Pages: 51-54**

---

## Introduction

The link between the مُسْنَدٌ and مُسْنَدٌ إِلَيْهِ can be restricted. This is called قَصْرٌ.

For example, the sentence, "Ahmad is happy" informs us regarding the link between Ahmad and happy. However, the sentence "Only Ahmad is happy" shows that the quality of being happy only applies to Ahmad, no one else.

---

## Parts of a قَصْرٌ

A قَصْرٌ comprises of 3 elements.

### 1. مَقْصُوْرٌ

This is the item or quality being restricted. e.g., happiness.

### 2. مَقْصُوْرٌ عَلَيْهِ

This is the item or quality the مَقْصُوْرٌ is being restricted to. e.g., Ahmad.

### 3. أَدَاةُ قَصْرٍ

This is the word used to create the قَصْرٌ. e.g., only

---

## Relationship Between the مَقْصُوْرٌ and مَقْصُوْرٌ عَلَيْهِ

*(Page 52)*

There are two types of relationships between the مَقْصُوْرٌ and مَقْصُوْرٌ عَلَيْهِ.

---

### قَصْرُ الصِّفَةِ عَلَى الْمَوْصُوْفِ (Restricting a Quality to a Being)

Restricting a quality, صِفَةٌ, to one person or being, مَوْصُوْفٌ.

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿لَآ إِلٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ﴾

</div>

> *There is **no** god **but** Allah.*

In this verse, the quality of being a god is restricted to a single being, Allah.

This is called قَصْرُ الصِّفَةِ عَلَى الْمَوْصُوفِ.

---

### قَصْرُ الْمَوْصُوْفِ عَلَى الصِّفَةِ (Restricting a Being to a Quality)

Restricting a person or thing, مَوْصُوْفٌ, to one quality, صِفَةٌ.

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿وَمَا مُحَمَّدٌ إِلَّا رَسُوْلٌ﴾

</div>

> ***Muhammad** is **but** a messenger.*

In this verse, a person, Muhammad (peace and blessings be upon him), is restricted to one quality.

This is called قَصْرُ الْمَوْصُوْفِ عَلَى الصِّفَةِ.

---

## The Scope of Qasr

*(Page 53)*

There are two types of قَصْرٌ.

---

### قَصْرٌ حَقِيْقِيٌّ (Qasr Haqiqi - Absolute Restriction)

قَصْرٌ حَقِيْقِيٌّ is an absolute restriction.

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿لَآ إِلٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ﴾

</div>

> *There is **no** god **but** Allah.*

In this verse the restriction of god to a single being is absolute i.e. there is no god but Allah.

---

### قَصْرٌ إِضَافِيٌّ (Qasr Idafi - Relative Restriction)

قَصْرٌ إِضَافِيٌّ is a relative restriction.

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿وَمَا مُحَمَّدٌ إِلَّا رَسُوْلٌ﴾

</div>

> ***Muhammad** (peace and blessings be upon him) is **but** a messenger.*

In this example, the restriction is relative, not absolute. i.e. he is only a prophet, not an eternal being.

---

### Note

قَصْرُ الْمَوْصُوْفِ عَلَى الصِّفَةِ will always be إضافي because no being can have only one quality.

---

## Ways of Creating قصر

*(Page 54)*

There are different ways of restricting:

---

### إِلَّا (Illa)

Using a negative particle followed by the particle إِلَّا.

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿وَمَا مُحَمَّدٌ إِلَّا رَسُوْلٌ﴾

</div>

> *Muhammad (peace and blessings be upon him) is **but** a messenger.*

---

### إِنَّمَا (Innama)

Using the particle إِنَّمَا.

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿قَالُوْا إِنَّمَا نَحْنُ مُصْلِحُوْنَ ۞﴾

</div>

> *We are **but** reformers.*

---

### بَلْ and لٰكِنْ (Bal and Lakin)

Using the بَلْ of حَرْفُ الْعَطْفِ.

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿وَقَالُوْا قُلُوْبُنَا غُلْفٌ بَلْ لَعَنَهُمُ اللهُ بِكُفْرِهِمْ فَقَلِيْلًا مَا يُؤْمِنُوْنَ ۞﴾

</div>

> *They said: "Our hearts are sealed" - **rather**, Allah has set a seal over them because of their disbelief*

Using the لٰكِنْ of حَرْفُ الْعَطْفِ.

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿وَمَا قَتَلُوْهُ وَمَا صَلَبُوْهُ وَلٰكِنْ شُبِّهَ لَهُمْ﴾

</div>

> *They did not kill him, nor crucify him, **but** they were deluded by resemblance.*

---

### تَقْدِيْمٌ (Taqdim - Fronting)

Fronting a particular part of the sentence.

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِيْنُ ۞﴾

</div>

> ***You alone** do we worship, and **from You alone** do we seek help.*

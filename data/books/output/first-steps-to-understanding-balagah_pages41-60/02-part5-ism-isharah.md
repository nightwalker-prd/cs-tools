# Part 5 (cont.) -- اسْمُ إِشَارَةٍ (Ism Isharah - Demonstrative Pronoun)

**Unit 1: علم المعاني (Ilm al-Ma'ani)**
**Page: 42**

---

## Definition

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

اسْمُ إِشَارَةٍ

</div>

An اسْمُ إِشَارَةٍ is primarily used to point to something.

Sometimes it can used for other reasons, some of which are mentioned below.

---

## Secondary Usages of اسْمُ إِشَارَةٍ

### تَعْظِيْمٌ (Ta'dhim - Showing Grandeur)

To show the grandeur of the thing being spoken about.

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿الٓمٓ ۞ ذٰلِكَ الْكِتٰبُ لَا رَيْبَ فِيْهِ هُدًى لِّلْمُتَّقِيْنَ ۞﴾

</div>

> ***This** Book*

---

### تَحْقِيْرٌ (Tahqir - Showing Insignificance)

To show the insignificance of the thing being spoken about.

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿أَهٰذَا الَّذِىْ يَذْكُرُ اٰلِهَتَكُمْ﴾

</div>

> *Is **this** the one who talks of your gods?*

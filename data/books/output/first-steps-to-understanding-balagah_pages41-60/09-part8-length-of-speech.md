# Part 8 -- طُوْلُ الكَلَامِ (Length of Speech)

**Unit 1: علم المعاني (Ilm al-Ma'ani)**
**Pages: 57-60**

---

## Introduction

A speaker may express his idea in one of three ways:

1. **مُسَاوَاةٌ**: a moderate way which is neither too brief nor too wordy.
2. **إِيْجَازٌ**: a concise manner expressing a meaning with lesser words than would usually be required.
3. **إِطْنَابٌ**: a lengthy manner expressing a meaning in more words than would usually be required.

### Overview Chart

| Succinct | | Wordy |
|:--------:|:---:|:-----:|
| إِيْجَازٌ | مُسَاوَاةٌ | إِطْنَابٌ |

---

## مُسَاوَاةٌ (Musawah - Moderation)

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

مُسَاوَاةٌ

</div>

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿وَمَنْ يُطِعِ اللهَ وَرَسُوْلَهُ يُدْخِلْهُ جَنّٰتٍ تَجْرِيْ مِنْ تَحْتِهَا الْأَنْهٰرُ خٰلِدِيْنَ فِيْهَا وَذٰلِكَ الْفَوْزُ الْعَظِيْمُ ۞﴾

</div>

> *Whoever obeys Allah and His Messenger, He will admit him to gardens beneath which rivers flow, where he will live forever. That is a great success.*

---

## إِيْجَازٌ (Ijaz - Conciseness)

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

إِيْجَازٌ

</div>

*(Page 58)*

There are two types of إِيْجَاز:

1. إِيْجَازُ قَصْرٍ
2. إِيْجَازُ حَذْفٍ

---

### إِيْجَازُ قَصْرٍ (Ijaz Qasr - Conciseness by Brevity)

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

إِيْجَازُ قَصْرٍ

</div>

إِيْجَازُ قَصْرٍ is expressing an idea using relatively few words.

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿وَلَكُمْ فِيْهَا مَا تَشْتَهِيْ أَنْفُسُكُمْ وَلَكُمْ فِيْهَا مَا تَدَّعُوْنَ ۞﴾

</div>

> *And for you in Heaven is whatever your souls desire.*

The words within this verse are few, but its meaning is very extensive.

---

### إِيْجَازُ حَذْفٍ (Ijaz Hadhf - Conciseness by Omission)

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

إِيْجَازُ حَذْفٍ

</div>

إِيْجَازُ حَذْفٍ is to remove something from the text.

This is of two types:

1. حَذْفُ اخْتِصَارٍ
2. حَذْفُ اقْتِصَارٍ

---

### حَذْفُ اخْتِصَارٍ (Hadhf Ikhtisar - Omission by Abbreviation)

*(Page 59)*

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

حَذْفُ اخْتِصَارٍ

</div>

حَذْفُ اخْتِصَارٍ is removing a word, words, phrase or sentence which have a grammatical function in the sentence from which it was omitted.

For example, a جُمْلَةٌ اسْمِيَّةٌ is comprised of a مُبْتَدَأٌ and خَبَرٌ. One of these can be removed if the context allows.

Below, are some types of حَذْفُ اخْتِصَارٍ.

#### 1) مُبْتَدَأٌ (Mubtada' - Subject)

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿وَمَآ أَدْرٰكَ مَا هِيَهْ ۞ ـــ نَارٌ حَامِيَةٌ ۞﴾

</div>

> *And what may let you know what that is? [It is] A blazing Fire!*

#### 2) خَبَرٌ (Khabar - Predicate)

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿أُكُلُهَا دَآئِمٌ وَظِلُّهَا ـــ ﴾

</div>

> *Its food is everlasting and its shade [is also everlasting].*

#### 3) فِعْلٌ (Fi'l - Verb)

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿وَلَئِنْ سَأَلْتَهُمْ مَنْ خَلَقَ السَّمٰوٰتِ وَالْأَرْضَ وَسَخَّرَ الشَّمْسَ وَالْقَمَرَ لَيَقُوْلُنَّ ـــ اللهُ﴾

</div>

> *And if you ask them: Who created the heavens and the earth, and who subjugated the sun and the moon. They will say: Allah **(created and subjugated them)**.*

The verb أَذْكُرُ is commonly omitted.

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿وَ ـــ إِذْ قَالَ مُوْسَىٰ لِقَوْمِهِ﴾

</div>

> *And (remember) when Musa said to his people ...*

#### 4) فَاعِلٌ (Fa'il - Subject/Doer)

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿كَلَّآ إِذَا بَلَغَتْ ـــ التَّرَاقِيَ ۞﴾

</div>

> *When [the soul] reaches to the collar bone.*

#### 5) مَفْعُوْلٌ بِهِ (Maf'ul bihi - Object)

*(Page 60)*

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿فَذُوْقُوْا ـــ فَلَنْ تَزِيْدَكُمْ إِلَّا عَذَابًا ۞﴾

</div>

> *Taste (**the punishment**), for we will only increase you in punishment.*

#### 6) حَرْفٌ (Harf - Particle)

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿قَالُوْا تَاللهِ ـــ تَفْتَؤُا تَذْكُرُ يُوْسُفَ﴾

</div>

> *By God, you will not stop remembering Yusuf.*

تَفْتَأُ is usually used in the negative i.e., مَا يَنْفَكُّ or لَا يَفْتَأُ.

#### 7) مُضَافٌ (Mudaf - Possessive)

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿وَسْئَلِ ـــ الْقَرْيَةَ الَّتِيْ كُنَّا فِيْهَا﴾

</div>

> *Ask [**the people of**] the town.*

#### 8) مَنْعُوْتٌ (Man'ut - Described Noun)

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿وَعِنْدَهُمْ ـــ قٰصِرٰتُ الطَّرْفِ عِيْنٌ ۞﴾

</div>

> *And by their side there will be (**wives**) lowering their gazes.*

#### 9) نَعْتٌ (Na't - Adjective)

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

لَا صَلَاةَ لِمَنْ لَمْ يَقْرَأْ بِفَاتِحَةِ الْكِتَابِ

</div>

> *There is no **complete** salah without the recitation of al-Fatihah.*

#### 10) جَوَابُ الشَّرْطِ (Jawab al-Shart - Response of the Conditional)

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿قُلْ أَرَءَيْتُمْ إِنْ كَانَ مِنْ عِنْدِ اللهِ ثُمَّ كَفَرْتُمْ بِهِ ـــ مَنْ أَضَلُّ مِمَّنْ هُوَ فِيْ شِقَاقٍ بَعِيْدٍ ۞﴾

</div>

> *"Tell me, if it is from Allah, and still you reject it..."*

#### 11) جُمْلَةٌ (Jumlah - Full Sentence)

<div dir="rtl" lang="ar">

﴿إِنِّيْ أَمَنْتُ بِرَبِّكُمْ فَاسْمَعُوْنِ ۞ ـــ قِيْلَ ادْخُلِ الْجَنَّةَ قَالَ يٰلَيْتَ قَوْمِيْ يَعْلَمُوْنَ ۞ بِمَا غَفَرَ لِيْ رَبِّيْ وَجَعَلَنِيْ مِنَ الْمُكْرَمِيْنَ ۞﴾

</div>

> *[Continues with the story of the believer who was martyred and then admitted to Paradise]*

## Introduction (p. 70)

The sub-science of عِلْمُ الْبَيَانِ deals with the aspects of Arabic language that are related to the following three topics:

1. تَشْبِيهٌ : similes
2. مَجَازٌ : metaphors
3. كِنَايَةٌ : allegories

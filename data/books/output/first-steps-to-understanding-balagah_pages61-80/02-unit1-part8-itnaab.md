# Unit 1: علم المعاني (Ilm al-Ma'ani) -- Part 8 (Continued)
# الإِطْنَابُ (Al-Itnaab / Elaboration)

> Pages 63-65

---

<div dir="rtl">

**الإِطْنَابُ**

</div>

الإِطْنَابُ occurs in various ways. Some are as follows:

---

## ذِكْرُ الْخَاصِّ بَعْدَ الْعَامِّ (Mentioning the Specific After the General)

<div dir="rtl">

**ذِكْرُ الْخَاصِّ بَعْدَ الْعَامِّ**

</div>

ذِكْرُ الْخَاصِّ بَعْدَ الْعَامِّ is to mention something specifically after something general which already includes it.

> <div dir="rtl">
>
> ﴿حَافِظُوا عَلَى الصَّلَوَاتِ وَالصَّلَاةِ الْوُسْطَى﴾
>
> </div>
>
> *Take due care of all the prayers, and the middle prayer.*

The word الصَّلَوَاتِ includes all prayers, but الصَّلَاةِ الْوُسْطَى is mentioned specifically to emphasise its importance.

---

## ذِكْرُ الْعَامِّ بَعْدَ الْخَاصِّ (Mentioning the General After the Specific)

<div dir="rtl">

**ذِكْرُ الْعَامِّ بَعْدَ الْخَاصِّ**

</div>

ذِكْرُ الْعَامِّ بَعْدَ الْخَاصِّ is to mention something generally even though it has been mentioned specifically before.

> <div dir="rtl">
>
> ﴿رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي وَلِوَالِدَيَّ وَلِمَنْ دَخَلَ بَيْتِيَ مُؤْمِنًا وَلِلْمُؤْمِنِينَ وَالْمُؤْمِنَاتِ﴾
>
> </div>
>
> *My Lord, grant pardon to me, and to my parents, and to everyone who enters my home as a believer, and to all the believing men and believing women.*

---

## تَكْرَارٌ (Takraar / Repetition)

<div dir="rtl">

**تَكْرَارٌ**

</div>

تَكْرَارٌ is to repeat a word or sentence.

> <div dir="rtl">
>
> ﴿فَبِأَيِّ آلَاءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ﴾
>
> </div>
>
> *So, which of the bounties of your Lord will you deny?*

This verse is mentioned 31 times in the chapter.

---

## تَذْيِيلٌ (Tadhyeel / Appending)

<div dir="rtl">

**تَذْيِيلٌ**

</div>

تَذْيِيلٌ is to bring two sentences, one after the other, with the second emphasising the meaning of the first.

> <div dir="rtl">
>
> ﴿وَقُلْ جَاءَ الْحَقُّ وَزَهَقَ الْبَاطِلُ إِنَّ الْبَاطِلَ كَانَ زَهُوقًا﴾
>
> </div>
>
> *Truth has come and falsehood has vanished. Falsehood is surely bound to vanish.*

---

## تَتْمِيمٌ (Tatmeem / Completing)

<div dir="rtl">

**تَتْمِيمٌ**

</div>

تَتْمِيمٌ is to add a word which highlights the meaning of the sentence.

> <div dir="rtl">
>
> ﴿وَيُطْعِمُونَ الطَّعَامَ عَلَى حُبِّهِ مِسْكِينًا وَيَتِيمًا وَأَسِيرًا﴾
>
> </div>
>
> *And they give food, out of their love for Him (Allah), to the needy, and the orphan, and the captive.*

The words عَلَى حُبِّهِ highlights the virtue of their spending.

---

## اعْتِرَاضٌ (I'tiraad / Parenthetical Insertion)

<div dir="rtl">

**اعْتِرَاضٌ**

</div>

اعْتِرَاضٌ is to bring a sentence within another sentence.

> <div dir="rtl">
>
> ﴿وَيَجْعَلُونَ لِلَّهِ الْبَنَاتِ سُبْحَانَهُ وَلَهُمْ مَا يَشْتَهُونَ﴾
>
> </div>
>
> *They ascribe daughters to Allah - Pure is He - and for themselves is what they desire!*

---

## اِحْتِرَاسٌ (Ihtiraas / Precautionary Clarification)

<div dir="rtl">

**اِحْتِرَاسٌ**

</div>

اِحْتِرَاسٌ is to bring a word, phrase or sentence within another sentence to remove any misconception.

> <div dir="rtl">
>
> ﴿وَاضْمُمْ يَدَكَ إِلَى جَنَاحِكَ تَخْرُجْ بَيْضَاءَ مِنْ غَيْرِ سُوءٍ﴾
>
> </div>
>
> *Press your hand under your arm, and it will come out [brightly] white without any disease.*

The phrase مِنْ غَيْرِ سُوءٍ prevents someone interpreting the whiteness of his hand as an illness.

---

## التَّوْضِيحُ بَعْدَ الْإِبْهَامِ (At-Tawdeeh Ba'dal Ibhaam / Clarification After Ambiguity)

<div dir="rtl">

**التَّوْضِيحُ بَعْدَ الْإِبْهَامِ**

</div>

التَّوْضِيحُ بَعْدَ الْإِبْهَامِ is to bring a word or pronoun ambiguously followed by its clarification.

Two occasions where this occurs are as follows:

### 1. ضَمِيرُ الشَّأْنِ (Dameer ash-Sha'n / Pronoun of Importance)

This is a pronoun which is brought at the beginning of the sentence without an antecedent.

> <div dir="rtl">
>
> ﴿قُلْ هُوَ اللَّهُ أَحَدٌ﴾
>
> </div>
>
> *Say: The truth is that Allah is One.*

### 2. التَّمْيِيزُ الْمُحَوَّلُ (At-Tamyeez al-Muhawwal / Converted Specification)

This is a تَمْيِيزٌ which was originally another slot in a sentence.

> <div dir="rtl">
>
> ﴿وَاشْتَعَلَ الرَّأْسُ شَيْبًا﴾
>
> </div>
>
> *And my head has lit ablaze with grey hair.*

In this verse, شَيْبًا is a تَمْيِيزٌ. Outside of the Quran, it would have been the فَاعِلٌ and the sentence would have read as follows:

> <div dir="rtl">
>
> اِشْتَعَلَ شَيْبُ الرَّأْسِ
>
> </div>
>
> *The white hair in my head has lit ablaze.*

This modified structure creates emphasis by attributing the blaze to the entire head, and not simply the hair.

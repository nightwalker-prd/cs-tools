## 1. Basic Sentence Structure

### جُمْلَة (Sentence)

Arabic has two types of sentences:
- **جُمْلَة اسْمِيَّة** (Nominal Sentence) - begins with a noun
- **جُمْلَة فِعْلِيَّة** (Verbal Sentence) - begins with a verb

### الجُمْلَة الفِعْلِيَّة Components

| Component | Arabic | Function |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Verb | فِعْل | The action (ماضٍ / مُضارِع / مَجْهُول) |
| Subject | فَاعِل | Who performs the action |
| Object | مَفْعُول بِهِ | What receives the action |
| Adverbs | مَفْعُولات | Additional information |

## 3. مَنْصُوب Slots (Accusative)

| Arabic | English | Translation | Source |
|--------|---------|-------------|--------|
| **مَفْعُول بِهِ** | Object | - | - |
| **مَفْعُول بِهِ ثانٍ** | Second Object | - | - |
| **مَفْعُول فِيه** | Adverb of Time/Place | - | - |
| **مَفْعُول مُطْلَق** | Emphasis | very, extremely | مَصْدَر of main verb |
| **مَفْعُول لَهُ** | Reason | because of, due to | مَصْدَر of another verb |
| **حَال** | State | -ing, -ly | نَكِرَة |
| **تَمْيِيز** | Clarification | with, in, of | نَكِرَة |
| **مُسْتَثْنَى** | Exclusion | except | After إلَّا |

### Detailed Explanations

#### مَفْعُول مُطْلَق vs مَفْعُول لَهُ

| Type | Source | Example |
|------|--------|---------|
| مَفْعُول مُطْلَق | مَصْدَر of **same** verb | صَبَرَ صَبْرًا (was very patient) |
| مَفْعُول لَهُ | مَصْدَر of **different** verb | صَبَرَ رَغْبَةً (was patient in hope) |

#### حَال (State)

- Describes condition at time of action
- Translated with -ing or -ly
- Agrees in number and gender with the noun it describes
- Pattern: اسْمُ الفَاعِل or اسْمُ المَفْعُول

#### تَمْيِيز (Clarification)

- Clarifies ambiguity
- Translated as: with, in, of
- Common with اسم التَفْضِيل

#### مُسْتَثْنَى (Exclusion)

- Word excluded from judgment
- Follows إلَّا
- مُسْتَثْنَى مِنْهُ must be mentioned

## 5. Emphasis Particles

### قَدْ (Particle of Verification)

| With | Translation Options |
|------|---------------------|
| **الماضِي** | indeed, certainly, has/have + past participle, emphatic "did" |
| **المُضَارِع** | indeed, certainly, OR sometimes/maybe (حَرْفُ تَقْلِيل) |

### لَامُ الابْتِدَاء

- Creates emphasis
- Can come before قَدْ for extra emphasis (لَقَدْ)
- With مُضَارِع: specifies to present tense

### نُونُ التَّأْكِيد (نّ)

- Strongest emphasis for مُضَارِع
- Formation: لَ + فِعْل مُضَارِع مَبْنِيّ + نَّ
- Translation: "will most certainly/definitely"
- Specifies to future tense

### لَنْ (Emphatic Negation)

- Negates مُضَارِع emphatically
- Makes مُضَارِع مَنْصُوب
- Specifies to future tense
- Translation: "will not"

### Summary Table: Verb Emphasis by Tense

| Tense | Affirmative | Negative |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Past** | قَدْ | مَا / قَطْ |
| **Present** | لَامُ الابْتِدَاء | لَا |
| **Future** | لَامُ الابْتِدَاء + نُونُ التَّأْكِيد | لَنْ |

## 6. Word Order and Fronting

### مُقَدَّم (Fronted Element)

- Any element brought before its usual position
- Creates emphasis on that element
- Labelled as مُقَدَّم in tarkib

### Translation of Emphasis

1. **Underlining/Italics**: Muslims worship _Allah_.
2. **Using "only"**: The Muslims worship only Allah.
3. **Restructuring**: It is Allah who the Muslims worship.

### Important Rule

If sentence starts with مَنْصُوب word → مَفْعُول مُقَدَّم (not مُبْتَدَأ)

## 7. إلَّا: Exclusion vs Emphasis

### Quick Reference

| Condition | مُسْتَثْنَى مِنْهُ | Label | Purpose | Translation |
|-----------|-----------------|-------|---------|-------------|
| Affirmative | Present | حَرْف اسْتِثْنَاء | Exclusion | except |
| Negative | Present | حَرْف اسْتِثْنَاء | Exclusion | except |
| Negative | Absent | حَرْف حَصْر | Emphasis | only |

### Examples

| Type | Arabic | English |
|------|--------|---------|
| Exclusion | جَاءَ الرِّجَالُ إلَّا زَيْدًا | The men came except Zaid |
| Emphasis | مَا جَاءَ إلَّا زَيْدٌ | Only Zaid came |

### Emphasising the فَاعِل

When فَاعِل is emphasized with إلَّا:
- فَاعِل comes after إلَّا
- Labelled as فَاعِل مُؤَخَّر
- Verb remains masculine even with feminine فَاعِل

## Complete Tarkib Template

```
[لَامُ الابْتِدَاء] [حَرْف نَفْي] [حَرْف تَحْقِيق] [فِعْل] [فَاعِل] [مَفْعُول بِهِ] [مَفْعُول مُطْلَق/لَهُ] [حَال] [تَمْيِيز] [حَرْف اسْتِثْنَاء/حَصْر] [مُسْتَثْنَى]
```

### Example Tarkib

**Sentence:** لَيَنْصُرَنَّ اللهُ المُسْلِمِينَ نَصْرًا عَظِيمًا

| Element | Arabic | Label |
|---------|--------|-------|
| لَ | لَ | لَامُ الابْتِدَاء |
| يَنْصُرَ | يَنْصُرَ | فِعْل مُضَارِع مَبْنِيّ |
| نَّ | نَّ | نُونُ التَّأْكِيد |
| اللهُ | اللهُ | فَاعِل |
| المُسْلِمِينَ | المُسْلِمِينَ | مَفْعُول بِهِ |
| نَصْرًا | نَصْرًا | مَفْعُول مُطْلَق |
| عَظِيمًا | عَظِيمًا | صِفَة |

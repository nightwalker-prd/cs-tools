## Overview

Arabic provides multiple ways to emphasize different parts of a sentence. The main categories are:

| Type | Arabic Term | Description |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| Verbal Emphasis | تَأْكِيدٌ لَفْظِيٌّ | Emphasis through word repetition |
| Semantic Emphasis | تَأْكِيدٌ مَعْنَوِيٌّ | Emphasis through specific emphatic words |

---

## Emphasis of Nouns

Nouns can be emphasized in several ways:

### 1. Fronting (التقديم)

Using **إِلَّا** (illā) - "except/only" construction to emphasize a noun by fronting it.

**Example:**
- إِيَّاهُ إِيَّاهُ نَسْتَعِينُ — "Him alone, Him alone we seek help from"

### 2. Verbal Emphasis (تَأْكِيدٌ لَفْظِيٌّ)

Repeating the same word to emphasize it.

**Example:**
- غَابَ أَحْمَدُ أَحْمَدُ — "Ahmad, Ahmad was absent" (emphasizing Ahmad)

### 3. Semantic Emphasis (تَأْكِيدٌ مَعْنَوِيٌّ)

Using specific emphatic words. The structure is: **مُضَافٌ – مُضَافٌ إِلَيْهِ** (possessive construction)

#### Emphatic Words for Individuals:

| Arabic | Meaning | Usage |
|--------|---------|-------|
| عَيْن | self/very | For singular emphasis |
| نَفْس | self/very | For singular emphasis |

**Examples:**
- حَضَرَ الأُسْتَاذُ نَفْسُهُ — "The teacher himself attended"
- حَضَرَتِ المُعَلِّمَةُ عَيْنُهَا — "The (female) teacher herself attended"

#### Emphatic Words for Quantity (Quantifiers):

| Arabic | Meaning | Usage |
|--------|---------|-------|
| كِلَا | both (masculine) | For dual |
| كِلْتَا | both (feminine) | For dual |
| كُلّ | all/every | For plural |
| جَمِيع | all | For plural |
| أَجْمَع | all together | For plural |

**Examples:**
- جَاءَ الأَخَوَانِ كِلَاهُمَا — "Both brothers came"
- ذَهَبَتِ البِنْتَانِ كِلْتَاهُمَا — "Both girls went"
- نُحِبُّ كُلَّ صَحَابِيٍّ — "We love every companion"
- أَذَّنَ المُؤَذِّنُونَ كُلُّهُمْ — "All the muezzins gave the call to prayer"
- وَصَلَ جَمِيعُ الرِّجَالِ — "All the men arrived"
- اعْتَمَرَتْ كِلْتَا الأُخْتَيْنِ — "Both sisters performed Umrah"
- أَذَّنَ المُؤَذِّنُونَ جَمِيعُهُمْ — "All the muezzins gave the call to prayer"
- أَذَّنَ المُؤَذِّنُونَ أَجْمَعُونَ — "All the muezzins together gave the call to prayer"

### 4. Exclusion Emphasis

Using **إِلَّا** with negation to emphasize:

**Example:**
- مَا غَابَ اليَوْمَ إِلَّا أَحْمَد — "No one was absent today except Ahmad" (emphasizing Ahmad)

## Emphasis of Adjectives

Adjectives can be emphasized through:

### 1. Verbal Emphasis (تَأْكِيدٌ لَفْظِيٌّ)

Repeating the adjective:

**Examples:**
- الفِيلُ كَبِيرٌ كَبِيرٌ — "The elephant is big, (very) big"
- الدَّمُ أَحْمَرُ أَحْمَرُ — "Blood is red, (very) red"
- الخَمْرُ حَرَامٌ حَرَامٌ — "Wine is forbidden, (absolutely) forbidden"
- المَوْتُ قَرِيبٌ قَرِيبٌ — "Death is near, (very) near"
- الشَّيْطَانُ رَجِيمٌ رَجِيمٌ — "Satan is accursed, (utterly) accursed"

### 2. Intensifier (جِدًّا)

Using **جِدًّا** (jiddan) meaning "very/extremely":

**Example:**
- الشَّجَرَةُ خَضْرَاءُ جِدًّا — "The tree is very green"

## Emphasis of Verbs

Different tenses have different emphatic structures:

### Past Tense Verbs

| Particle | Arabic | Effect |
|----------|--------|--------|
| قَدْ | qad | Emphasizes certainty |
| لَقَدْ | laqad | Stronger emphasis (with oath implied) |

**Examples:**
- قَدْ أَغْرَقَ اللهُ فِرْعَوْنَ — "Allah (certainly) drowned Pharaoh"
- لَقَدْ أَغْرَقَ اللهُ فِرْعَوْنَ — "Indeed, Allah drowned Pharaoh"

### Present Tense Verbs

| Particle | Arabic | Effect |
|----------|--------|--------|
| لَامُ الابْتِدَاءِ | Lām of beginning (لَـ) | Emphasizes the action |

**Example:**
- لَنَعْتَصِمُ بِكِتَابِ اللهِ — "We certainly adhere to the Book of Allah"

### Future Tense Verbs

#### Affirmative Future

Using **نُونُ التَّأْكِيدِ** (Nūn of Emphasis):

| Type | Arabic |
|------|--------|
| Heavy Nūn | ـَنَّ |
| Light Nūn | ـَنْ |

**Example:**
- لَنَتُوبَنَّ مِنَ الذُّنُوبِ — "We will certainly repent from sins"

#### Negative Future

Using **لَنْ** (lan) for emphatic negation:

**Example:**
- لَنْ نُضَيِّعَنَّ أَوْقَاتَنَا — "We will never waste our time"

### Verbal Emphasis (تَأْكِيدٌ لَفْظِيٌّ) for Verbs

Repeating the verb:

**Example:**
- نَعْتَصِمُ نَعْتَصِمُ بِالقُرْآنِ — "We adhere, (truly) adhere to the Quran"

| Part of Speech | Method | Example |
|----------------|--------|---------|
| **Noun** | Fronting with إِلَّا | مَا غَابَ إِلَّا أَحْمَد |
| **Noun** | Repetition | أَحْمَدُ أَحْمَدُ |

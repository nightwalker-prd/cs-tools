# Part 3: التَّرْكِيْبُ الْعَطْفِيُّ (Conjunctive Phrases)

## Definition

A **Conjunctive Phrase** (التَّرْكِيْبُ الْعَطْفِيُّ) consists of:
- **مَعْطُوْفٌ عَلَيْهِ** (Ma'ṭūf 'Alayh): The first noun/element
- **حَرْفُ الْعَطْفِ** (Ḥarf al-'Aṭf): The conjunction particle
- **مَعْطُوْفٌ** (Ma'ṭūf): The second noun/element (joined by the conjunction)

## Conjunction Particles (حُرُوْفُ الْعَطْفِ)

| Particle | Arabic | Meaning | Usage |
|----------|--------|---------|-------|
| وَ | وَاوُ الْعَطْفِ | and | General connection |
| فَ | فَاءُ الْعَطْفِ | then, so | Immediate sequence |
| ثُمَّ | ثُمَّ | then | Delayed sequence |
| أَوْ | أَوْ | or | Alternative/choice |
| بَلْ | بَلْ | rather, but | Correction |
| لَا | لَا | not | Negation of second element |
| لٰكِنْ | لٰكِنْ | but, however | Contrast |

## Basic Examples with وَ (And)

| Arabic | مَعْطُوْفٌ عَلَيْهِ | حَرْفُ الْعَطْفِ | مَعْطُوْفٌ | Translation |
|--------|------------------|----------------|----------|-------------|
| يَوْمٌ وَلَيْلَةٌ | يَوْمٌ | وَ | لَيْلَةٌ | A day and a night |
| سَنَةٌ وَشَهْرَانِ | سَنَةٌ | وَ | شَهْرَانِ | A year and two months |
| يَوْمَانِ وَلَيْلَتَانِ | يَوْمَانِ | وَ | لَيْلَتَانِ | Two days and two nights |
| الشَّمْسُ وَالْقَمَرُ | الشَّمْسُ | وَ | الْقَمَرُ | The sun and the moon |
| الْأَرْضُ وَالسَّمَاءُ | الْأَرْضُ | وَ | السَّمَاءُ | The earth and the sky |
| الْأَنْبِيَاءُ وَالرُّسُلُ | الْأَنْبِيَاءُ | وَ | الرُّسُلُ | The prophets and the messengers |
| الْمُسْلِمُوْنَ وَالْمُسْلِمَاتُ | الْمُسْلِمُوْنَ | وَ | الْمُسْلِمَاتُ | The Muslim men and women |

## Examples with أَوْ (Or)

| Arabic | Translation | Usage |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| مَاءٌ أَوْ لَبَنٌ | Water or milk | Choice between two |
| الْأَبُ أَوِ الْأُمُّ | The father or the mother | Alternative |
| تَمْرَةٌ أَوْ تَمْرَتَانِ | One date or two dates | Quantity choice |

## Examples with فَ (Then - Immediate)

| Arabic | Translation | Note |
|--------|-------------|------|
| الْأَخُ فَالْأُخْتَانِ | The brother then the two sisters | Immediate sequence |

## Examples with ثُمَّ (Then - Delayed)

| Arabic | Translation | Note |
|--------|-------------|------|
| مُحَمَّدٌ ثُمَّ خَالِدٌ | Muhammad then Khalid | Delayed sequence |

## The Agreement Rule

**Key Principle**: The مَعْطُوْفٌ (second noun) must agree with the مَعْطُوْفٌ عَلَيْهِ (first noun) in grammatical case (إِعْرَابٌ).

### Case Agreement Examples

| Case | Example | Analysis |
|------|---------|----------|
| Nominative (مَرْفُوْعٌ) | جَاءَ مُحَمَّدٌ وَخَالِدٌ | Both nominative |
| Accusative (مَنْصُوْبٌ) | رَأَيْتُ مُحَمَّدًا وَخَالِدًا | Both accusative |
| Genitive (مَجْرُوْرٌ) | مَرَرْتُ بِمُحَمَّدٍ وَخَالِدٍ | Both genitive |

**Note**: Unlike descriptive phrases, gender and number do NOT need to match - only case.

## Mixing Genders and Numbers

Unlike descriptive phrases, conjunctive phrases can join words of different genders and numbers, as long as the grammatical case matches:

| Arabic | Translation | Note |
|--------|-------------|------|
| الْأَبُ وَالْأُمُّ | The father and the mother | Masculine + Feminine |
| يَوْمٌ وَلَيْلَةٌ | A day and a night | Masculine + Feminine |
| الْمُسْلِمُوْنَ وَالْمُسْلِمَاتُ | Muslim men and women | Masculine pl. + Feminine pl. |
| سَنَةٌ وَشَهْرَانِ | A year and two months | Singular + Dual |

## Differences: وَ vs فَ vs ثُمَّ

| Particle | Time Relationship | Example |
|----------|-------------------|---------|
| وَ (and) | No specific order | الشَّمْسُ وَالْقَمَرُ |
| فَ (then) | Immediate sequence | دَخَلَ فَجَلَسَ (He entered then sat immediately) |
| ثُمَّ (then) | Delayed sequence | أَكَلَ ثُمَّ شَرِبَ (He ate then [later] drank) |

## Multiple Conjunctions

Phrases can have multiple conjunctions:

| Arabic | Translation |
|--------|-------------|
| الْأَبُ وَالْأُمُّ وَالْأَوْلَادُ | The father and the mother and the children |
| يَوْمٌ وَلَيْلَةٌ وَسَاعَةٌ | A day and a night and an hour |

## Summary Rules

1. **Structure**: مَعْطُوْفٌ عَلَيْهِ + حَرْفُ الْعَطْفِ + مَعْطُوْفٌ
2. **Case agreement**: Both nouns must have the same grammatical case
3. **Gender/Number**: Do NOT need to match (unlike descriptive phrases)
4. **Definiteness**: Each noun keeps its own definiteness status
5. **Common particles**: وَ (and), أَوْ (or), فَ (then-immediate), ثُمَّ (then-delayed)
6. **وَ indicates** general connection without time sequence
7. **فَ indicates** immediate sequence
8. **ثُمَّ indicates** delayed sequence
9. **أَوْ indicates** choice or alternative

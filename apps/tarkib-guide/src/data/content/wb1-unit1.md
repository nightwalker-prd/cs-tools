## Notes on Forms:

### Masculine (مُذَكَّر):
- **Singular**: Base form
- **Dual**: Add ـَانِ (nominative) / ـَيْنِ (accusative/genitive)
- **Plural**: Sound masculine plural adds ـُونَ (nominative) / ـِينَ (accusative/genitive)

### Feminine (مُؤَنَّث):
- **Singular**: Add ـَةٌ to masculine
- **Dual**: Add ـَتَانِ (nominative) / ـَتَيْنِ (accusative/genitive)
- **Plural**: Sound feminine plural adds ـَاتٌ

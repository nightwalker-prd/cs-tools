## Content Structure (4 Parts, 13 Topics, 39 Concepts)

1. **BBS, Usenet & Early File Sharing** — dial-up bulletin boards, newsgroup binaries, uuencoding, bandwidth economics
2. **Web 1.0 Monetization Pioneers** — first online credit card transactions, subscription paywalls, affiliate marketing networks
3. **Bandwidth & Compression Demand** — driving consumer broadband adoption, JPEG/MPEG optimization, progressive loading techniques

### Part 2: Streaming & Delivery Infrastructure (Topics 4-7)

4. **Streaming Video Pioneers** — pre-YouTube live streaming, adaptive bitrate origins, Flash video and early players
5. **Content Delivery Networks** — early CDN adoption at scale, edge caching strategies, geographic distribution for latency
6. **Peer-to-Peer Distribution** — BitTorrent adoption, distributed content delivery, hybrid P2P-CDN architectures
7. **Video Codec & Transcoding Innovation** — multi-bitrate encoding at scale, device-adaptive transcoding, real-time encoding pipelines

### Part 3: Payments, Privacy & Security (Topics 8-10)

8. **Online Payment Systems** — pioneering credit card processing on the web, recurring billing, age verification, fraud prevention
9. **Privacy & Anonymity Technologies** — VPN adoption drivers, anonymous payment methods, data minimization, user privacy as feature
10. **Anti-Piracy & DRM** — watermarking, fingerprinting, DMCA enforcement at scale, content protection economics

### Part 4: Modern Platform Engineering (Topics 11-13)

11. **Content Moderation at Scale** — AI-based classification, hash-matching (PhotoDNA/CSAM detection), trust & safety operations
12. **Recommendation & Personalization** — collaborative filtering, content tagging taxonomies, engagement optimization, A/B testing at scale
13. **Emerging Tech Adoption** — VR/AR as early adopters, interactive/haptic tech, AI-generated content, WebRTC for real-time interaction
